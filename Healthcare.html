Healthcare


So, you've just broken a bone. Where does that put you financially and medically?

The most important thing to do is to identify the difference between Healthcare and Health Insurance.

HEALTHCARE is the care given to the patient to restore their physical and mental well-being.

HEALTH INSURANCE is the financial support for the insured to cover medical expenses or cover losses due to things like illness or hospitalization. Health insurance often reflects the quality and quantity of the medical care provided to you.

Many people don't think about insurance as a priority when they are young because until age 26, most people can stay under their parents' insurance. Other people tend to get theirs from their employer or school. It is important to look through all options and get the one that works best for you.
