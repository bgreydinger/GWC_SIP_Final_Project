About Us


Hello, I'm Amrita! I am currently a student at Amazon's Girls Who Code program and am hoping to continue my passion for coding in college. My main mission in creating EduSphere was to build a collaborative and supportive community for high school students where ideas, advice, and helpful resources could be easily shared.



Hi, my name is Bianca and I am a girl who codes from Interlake High School. I have a strong passion for helping people become the best version of themselves. To help people along their life journey, I believed it would be extremely beneficial to create a website with information for high school and college students that is not taught in school. These life lessons are crucial to thrive in the adult world, and I strongly believe that this tool will help many young people on their journeys in college and beyond!
