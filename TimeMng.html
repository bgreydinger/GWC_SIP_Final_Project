Time Management


How am I supposed to be able to balance my school, home, and social life? There clearly isn't enough time in the day, right? Wrong. With these time management techniques you can go on to take back lost time and conquer your day.

1. Set goals that are measurable and achievable. Use the SMART method to ensure that yours goals are Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, Timely.

2. Prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance.

3. Take a break between tasks as it refreshes your mind and helps you see your work with a fresh mindset.

4. Plan Ahead. Start off each day with a list of tasks that must be done that day. End each night by writing out the tasks that need to be completed for the next day.

5. Block out distractions. Find a quiet working place free of distractions and channel your focus on the task ahead.
